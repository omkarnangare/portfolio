Social Activities

Punarjanani

AYUDH Chapter at Pune, kicked off their annual project ‘Punarjani’- The River Clean up and Maintenance Project on the World Water Day .

Green & Serene Environment Day

#AYUDHPune celebrated World Environment Day by building a beautiful garden at the Temple Premises.

Cleanliness Drive

AYUDH Pune team celebrated Shivaratri at Ghoradeshwar Temple by cleaning up the temple premises.

Swachatha Hi Seva SHS2018.

The 'Swachatha Hi Seva' movement in full swing. Members of AYUDH Pune joined hands to participate in the 'Swachatha Hi Seva' movement.The members including students and working professionals began cleaning from the foothills and proceeded up the hill. We cleaned the areas surrounding the Tukai and Shiva temple, and this was the place where we had conducted a tree plantation drive couple of months ago.

'ARPAN'

'ARPAN' , a special green initiative by AYUDH Pune commenced on the first day of Van Mahotsav (July 1 to 7). Inspired by Amma, AYUDHians decided to plant and maintain 65 Trees thereby converting a barren land to mini forest. This is an offering to our most beloved AMMA on Her 65th Birthday !!

Haritamritam

'Haritamritam' program at Mahindra Royale Society. The participants experienced their moment of delight by planting their own saplings The participants shared their feedback with the AYUDH team and expressed their gratitude on being part of this green initiative. They were highly impressed by the dedication of youth towards such an initiative and promised to look after the plants as their own children and send us the snips of the fruits they bear.
