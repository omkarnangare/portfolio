PUNARJANANI

  • by:Omkar Nangare
  • date:March 22, 2019

AYUDH Chapter at Pune, kicked off their annual project ‘Punarjani’- The River Clean up and Maintenance Project on the World Water Day 2019. Ayudhians did the clean up, installed dust bins on the river shore, planted native sapling on the river shore.

KEEP THE RIVERS CLEAN AND EARTH EVERGREEN

Shri A.N Tripathi. Mr. Tripathi, IFS Officer, Additional Principal Chief Conservator of Forest in Government of Maharashtra, in the rank of Additional Secretary in the Government of India was the chief guest. He addressed Ayudhians and inaugurated the initiative by joining for the clean up.
